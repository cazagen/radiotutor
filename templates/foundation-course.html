What is Amateur Radio?

Amateur Radio is a technical hobby for experimentation and self-learning of radio technology

Amateur Radio is a very broad hobby, covering loads of areas, including:

  • Communicating with Amateur Satellites
  • Electronics and Construction
  • Contesting
  • Amateur Radio Direction Finding
  • Emergency Communications
